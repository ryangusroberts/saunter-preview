The cost of not naming it

A miscast executive does not just underperform. They tax everyone around them.

Replacing a wrong-seat leader runs roughly 1.5 to 2x their salary. Leaving them there costs more: missed deadlines, a team quietly adjusting around the friction, and your strongest people starting to look. Naming it early is the cheapest move you have.
