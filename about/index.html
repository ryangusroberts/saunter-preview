About

Built by an operator who learned systems the hard way.

Fighter pilot, then failure modeler, then operator. Saunter turns hard-won discipline about how things break onto the businesses and leaders scaling under load.

Ryan "Gus" Roberts · Founder

Ryan spent years in environments where a missed failure mode was not a bad quarter, it was a catastrophe. He flew for the Air Force, modeled systemic risk for the Department of Defense, and built and sold a defense firm on that discipline.

Saunter exists because the same rigor that keeps aircraft and weapons systems safe is almost never applied to the businesses people bet their lives on. The framework is the product, and a trained team runs it, so the read does not depend on one person in the room.
